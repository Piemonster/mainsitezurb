Acoustic Keyboard Logger

It is possible, using a microphone, to capture the sound of keys on a QWERTY keyboard being pressed. These sounds can then be analyzed and an accurate reconstruction of the typed text can be built. We spent a weekend exploring how accurately we could do this. If you would like a copy of the training data we have then please email us.
